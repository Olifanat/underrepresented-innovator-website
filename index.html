Dr. Joy Buolamwini

About This Project

For Indiana University's Informatics 101 course, our team created a website highlighting an underrepresented innovator in computing, specifically in technology.

We chose Dr. Joy Buolamwini, a leading researcher and MIT graduate working in AI bias and founder of the Algorithmic Justice League.

Explore this site to learn about her work, our research process, and the team behind this project.
